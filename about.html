Wikimedia Community User Group Uganda

is a registered non-governmental organization and an official affiliate of the Wikimedia Foundation.

Since our founding in
2014 by a small group of passionate volunteers, we have grown into a vibrant community of over 500 members dedicated to making free knowledge accessible to every Ugandan. Based at Kanjokya House in Kampala, we bridge the gap between local culture and global information.

10+Years of Existence

Our Vision

A Uganda where every person has access to free knowledge in their own language and where local communities actively contribute to the world's largest collaborative knowledge projects

Our Mission

To promote free knowledge and digital literacy across Uganda by supporting Wikimedia projects, organizing community events, and empowering local contributors to share their knowledge with the world.

Our Core Values

Our work is guided by five foundational principles

Core Value Description
Volunteerism The heartbeat of our community
Adaptability Evolving with the digital landscape.
Inclusivity Ensuring every voice is heard.

Strategic Direction (2025–2029)

In 2025, we launched a transformative five-year strategic plan focused on four key pillars

OBJECTIVES FOCUS AREAS
Community Growth Expanding and diversifying our skilled volunteer base.
Content Excellence Enhancing the quality and volume of Ugandan-relevant content.
Advocacy Promoting policies that support equitable access to knowledge.
Sustainability Strengthening governance and diversifying funding for the future
